What Is ITB?

A platform built for ICT students

Intellectual Tech Bridge is designed to assist university students studying ICT and Technology. We provide past exam papers, learning modules, and a wide range of study resources — all under one roof, free of charge.

The website helps students prepare for tests and examinations, reinforcing key concepts and improving academic performance. Our platform is well-structured, allowing students to quickly find the materials they need, whenever they need them.

With new features like built-in study tools, exam tips, video resources, quizzes and progress tracking, ITB is more than just a download site — it's a complete study companion.

Applications Development

Programming fundamentals and software problem-solving

Applications Development focuses on how computer programs and software applications are created. It introduces students to the fundamental concepts of programming and how technology can be used to develop solutions for real-world problems.

Core topics include variables, algorithms, decision-making, logic operators and loops — the essential building blocks of any programming language.

VariablesAlgorithmsLoopsLogic OperatorsDecision-MakingProblem Solving
🌐

ICT Fundamentals

Core concepts in Information and Communication Technology

ICT Fundamentals walks students through the core ideas behind Information and Communication Technology — how data is generated, stored and transmitted across digital platforms.

The module covers hardware and software, networking, the Internet and communication tools including email and browsing techniques.

HardwareSoftwareNetworkingInternetEmail SystemsData Management
🖥️

Web Development

Building and publishing websites on the Internet

Web Development helps students learn how websites are created and used. Students learn how to place and organise information on a webpage — working with text, images and links so that content is clear and easy to navigate.

Web Development gives students digital skills that are highly relevant in today's world and is an excellent starting point for anyone interested in a technology career.

HTMLWeb PagesText & ImagesNavigationLinksWeb Projects
